Who We Are

We came up
in newsrooms.

QuickFlip Media was started by Samantha Morgan and Matthew White, two media professionals who built their careers in television newsrooms right here in Baton Rouge.

"You do not need the latest trend to get attention. You need a moment that actually matters."

After years in local news, they kept noticing the same thing. Organizations were doing great work every day, but could not find a way to show it. The stories were there. A system to tell them consistently was not.

In a newsroom, a story can go from idea to published in a single afternoon. You learn fast what is worth covering, how to work under pressure, and how to communicate clearly to people who are not paying close attention.

That is the discipline QuickFlip brings to businesses, nonprofits, and organizations that want to show up online without building a full production studio to do it.
